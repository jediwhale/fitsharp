The HTML parser respects <!--HTML comments -->, and none of the commented-out tables below should be recognized or executed.

The following cases are handled;

  • Tables entirely within comments
  • Tarnished tables, where the element is broken by comment prefix: <!--table> ... </table-->
  • Comments within tables, e.g. commented-out cells or rows
  • Unclosed comments
